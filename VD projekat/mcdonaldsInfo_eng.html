Our story

Every day, our twenty-seven McDonald's restaurants in Serbia visit tens of thousands of guests! As a leading fast-moving chain in the world and in Serbia, McDonald's is one of the world's largest brands, known for extremely high standards and synonymous with fast and friendly service, the highest quality products, clean and pleasant locations, that is, the value that a guest receives for his money - that is actually the formula of our success to which we are proud. Since March 1988, when the first McDonald's was opened in Slavia in Belgrade, to date, as many as twenty-seven McDonald's restaurants are operating throughout Serbia, we continuously invest in the development of our business, we are opening new jobs every year and improving our offer , so we strive to always provide our guests with the best.
